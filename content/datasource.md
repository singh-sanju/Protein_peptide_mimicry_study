## Data Sources

### Human Protein

Annexin (ANX)
Annexins are host proteins involved in membrane dynamics and immune processes. A peptide derived from Annexin was selected as the reference self-peptide for mimicry analysis.

### Microbial Source

Klebsiella pneumoniae proteome
K. pneumoniae has been frequently associated with ankylosing spondylitis and other HLA-B27â€“linked autoimmune conditions. Its complete proteome was therefore chosen as the microbial peptide source.
